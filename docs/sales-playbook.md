# Sales Playbook

## Main Offer Framing

We are not selling "AI" first. We are selling:

- faster response times
- more captured leads
- fewer missed inquiries
- better follow-up
- more booked appointments
- less repetitive admin work

## Ideal First Targets

- local service businesses
- clinics
- agencies
- small ecommerce brands
- real estate teams
- trades businesses

These businesses often have missed calls, slow website replies, weak follow-up, or no automation.

## Call Goal

The only goal of the first call is to win enough trust to book a meeting.

## Agent Style

- calm
- respectful
- direct
- slightly consultative
- never pushy
- confident without sounding fake

## Offer Angles

### Website chatbot

- capture visitors after hours
- answer FAQs instantly
- reduce missed leads

### Email automation

- faster replies
- fewer manual follow-ups
- cleaner lead routing

### AI voice sales

- answer or place calls automatically
- qualify leads before humans spend time
- recover missed opportunities

### Website automation or rebuild

- improve conversion
- create smoother booking and inquiry flows
- connect forms, chat, CRM, and follow-up

## Appointment CTA

Use simple language:

- "Would it make sense to show you a quick demo?"
- "If I can show you how this could help your website convert more leads, would you be open to a short call?"
- "Would a 15-minute chat with our team be useful next week?"

## Disqualifiers

- no need
- no decision authority
- wrong business fit
- strong no-interest signals
- no budget or no operational need
